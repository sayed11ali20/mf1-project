Subject

Reliability & Lifecycle Analysis

Asset

Remelt 1 - MF1 Skimming Door

Based on maintenance logs, the Mean Time Between Maintenance (MTBM) for MF1 components shows a Severe Deviation due to extreme heat stress.

Component Lifespan in Days (MTBM)

CRITICAL: Hydraulic Cylinders

Operational lifespan: 30-45 Days. Root cause: Rod seals melting due to direct thermal impact.

WARNING: Pneumatic Clamps

Lifespan: 90 Days. Failure mechanism: Bent piston rods and seal degradation.

Executive Conclusion

Management must prioritize a "Design Modification" strategy. Immediate action: installing robust thermal radiation shields for all actuation systems.
